About Carbyr

Carbyr will be providing car search for car enthusiats. Note the future tense: Carbyr is pre-launch!

Feeback welcomed

OK, more than welcomed; we want to help you find your next car. If we're doing something right, please let us know. If we are not yet providing what you need, tell us what to prioritize. If we goofed up, tell us so we can fix it! You can send us feedback via email. You can also reach us on twitter as @onegeelabs (the company behind the carbyr product).

Mind the mess!

Since Carbyr is pre-release, you may see some unexpected behavior. Listings may not update immediately, bad listings may be more prone to slipping through, etc. We apologize and hope that Carbyr still provides value. And please, send us that feedback! But we do have to say, for all you lawyers out there, that we cannot make any promises of merchantability. Use Carbyr at your own risk!

What's with all the miatas?

Ah, you noticed? :) When fully released, Carbyr will provide search for all cars. This early release (call it "alpha") site is miatas-only. Consider this a technology demonstration... and a great way to find what we think is the world's most iconic sportscar. If you want an rx-7 or a cx-9 (or even a 911), sign up to get notified when we open more functionality.
