About Carbyr

Carbyr will be providing car search for car enthusiats. Note the future tense: Carbyr is pre-launch!

Your feedback to this early release is encouraged. We want to help you find your next car. If we're doing something right, please let us know. If we are not yet providing what you need, tell us what to prioritize. If we goofed up, tell us so we can fix it! You can send us feedback via email. You can also reach us on twitter as @onegeelabs (the company behind the carbyr product).

What's with all the miatas?

Ah, you noticed? :) When fully released, Carbyr will provide search for all cars. This early release (call it "alpha") site is miatas-only. Consider this a technology demonstration... and a great way to find what we think is the world's most iconic sportscar.

If you want an rx-7 or a 911, send us a note at feedback@carbyr.com and we will send you a followup email when we open more functionality. Or, you can follow us on twitter @onegeelabs.

Tell me more!

Not good with waiting? :) Want to help us make carbyr better, faster? Want to get your cars into carbyr? Want to write about us?

You can email us or tweet us at @onegeelabs. Speaking of twitter, this is a semi-private invite. It's OK to share with your brother, but we're not quite ready for the whole world to land on us at once. Please do us the favor of not sharing widely about Carbyr on social media... yet!
