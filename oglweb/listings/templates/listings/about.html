About Carbyr

Carbyr will provide the world's best car search for car enthusiasts.

Note the future tense: Carbyr is in beta... we hope you love it, we think it's useful, and we're working to fulfill that "best" promise every day!

We want to be really clear about what we want to do for you -- and what we don't. So here goes:

Carbyr is NOT another classified site. The world has plenty of those. We don't take classified ad postings, and we don't charge sellers (or dealers) to list vehicles.

Carbyr is your car search hub. We index car listings from across the web and share them here for the convenience of YOU, the buyer.

One Carbyr search scours classified and auction sites, enthusiast forums, and dealership inventories -- more interesting cars than any other single site or app.

Note that word "interesting." Carbyr is a Camry-free zone. No car appliances here. We are enthusiasts, and if you are reading this we bet you love cars too. We have left out some of the high-volume blandmobiles to reduce the size of the haystack, and we are committed to building the best search tools to help you sift out a specific needle (to torture the metaphor) to put in your garage.
