2006 Bentley Continental

Xenon Lamps, iPod integration, Navigation - 55,317 Miles

This 2006 Bentley Continental GT for sale is finished in Black with Light interior. In typical Bentley form she has all the right equipment including Xenon Lamps, iPod integration, Navigation, Front and Rear Parking sensors, Heated Seats, Diamond Stitched Seats, Wood Veneer Wheel and more. Having traveled only 55,317 miles, this well maintained example just completed service, including fresh brakes at the local Bentley dealership. Her stock wheels were custom painted in black and finished with red pinstripe but retain factory fitment (Factory Mulliner Wheels are now silver as shown in the slideshow below). From her powerful W12 motor to her refined finishes this hand built car is the perfect example of the finest Crewe has to offer.

We are asking $59,995 for this hand built Grand Tourer.
