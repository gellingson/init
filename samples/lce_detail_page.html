2011 Rolls-Royce Phantom

Spirit of Ecstasy Centenary, 1 of 100

Beautiful 2011 Rolls Royce available from Left Coast Exotics. This was an unique year for Rolls Royce, as it celebrated 100 years of its legendary mascot, the Spirit of Ecstasy. To mark the occasion, Rolls produced an unique run of celebratory Phantoms.

This special edition is distinguished from the remainder of the Phantoms you'll see with a collection of distinct touches. This particular instance is finished in Ethereal White Pearl and Sea Shell White Interior with Tropical Veneer and Dark Wenge/Ebony. The inside also provides a variety of special accents only found on this quite limited production car. They are:

Cast in silver, each Spirit of Ecstasy provided a base total with black-gold plated bezel bearing the celebratory engraving "Spirit of Ecstasy Centenary - 2011", a tale duplicated on the cover plate when the mascot is reduced. The style is finished with a set of six characteristics, 2 of which have been created specifically for Rolls-Royce.

A new badge was developed to authenticate the Collection. Located below the Spirit of Ecstasy, on the trunk cover as well as on front side panels, the badge reverses traditional style, with white enamel RR information set on a background of black chrome.

A hallmark Rolls-Royce twin coachline is accentuated on these Collectible cars by a hand-painted Spirit of Ecstasy regalia, the inspiration of which was drawn from an initial line-drawing by Charles Sykes, the mascot's creator.

The glove box lid carries an initial Sykes quote embossed into the color-matched leather lining: "A graceful little goddess, the Spirit of Ecstasy, who has selected road travel as her supreme delight and alighted on the prow of a Rolls-Royce motor car to revel in the freshness of the air and the musical sound of her fluttering draperies." -- Charles Sykes, 1911.

An analog clock fitted in the fascia panel, the external bezel of which is crafted from Britannia Silver, incorporating the one-of-a-kind collection of 6 trademarks. The backlit face offers an internal ring of silver integrating the centenary lettering.

Instrument dials match the clock style. Satin spun steel dials, focused with polished RR monograms are additionally enhanced with refined chaplets and black characters.

The lid of the center console discloses a strong Britannia Silver commemorative plaque etched with the lettering "Spirit of Ecstasy - One of One Hundred".

4 special exterior color alternatives were provided: (1) Rhapsody Black, a black metallic integrating a gold sparkle, (2) Maiden Blue, a deep blue metallic with a silver glimmer, (3) Ethereal White Pearl, with a special pearlescent finish, (4) Wildflower, an abundant blend of brown and red shade with a great shimmer.

Hand crafted banding of Britannia Silver was inset into the veneer along with a choice of natural leather and indoor contrast schemes.

This specific example is in impressive condition and outfitted with HD radio, iPod combination, satellite, navigation, starburst headliner, Complete rear curtains in oyster, camera system, voice processing, exposed twin exhaust tips, together with all the one-of-a-kind Spirit of Ecstasy touches stated above.
