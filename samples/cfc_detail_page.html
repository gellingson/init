2011 Toyota FJ                                   
   The Toyota FJ Cruiser is a retro style SUV 4X4 with styling and off road performance reminiscent of the original Toyota FJ 40 Land Cruisers Introduced as a concept car in January 2003 the FJ Cruiser was approved for production after positive consumer response and debuted in 2005 in final production form.  On 5 November 2013 Toyota announced in that the 2014 model year would be the last for the FJ Cruiser.  This is a stunning example of a 2011 Toyota FJ. Only one owner and only 36,300 original miles, there were only 13,541 produced and imported into the US.  It has a clean title and clear car fax.  It is finished in Silver Fresco Metallic with Dark Charcoal interior, fully equipped including the compass package,  5-speed automatic, all power options and more. Powered by a 4.0L DOHC V6 with 260 hp.  #112399, Now on display at the home of “over the top cars”
